Our Story

Furniture Built on
Passion & Purpose

CINTER was born from a single workshop in Cape Town with one belief: furniture should tell a story. For over 18 years, we have been transforming sustainably sourced South African timber into heirloom pieces that define spaces and outlast trends.

Every joint is hand-fitted. Every finish is applied by hand. From the sprawling office desk to the intimate bedside table, CINTER pieces carry the warmth of the wood and the integrity of the craftsman who shaped it.
